SDP Projects

The SDP courses teach students problem solving, critical thinking, project management, and teamwork skills. These help students in their other courses, projects, and career.

EDI Projects

Engineering Design and Innovation (EDI) typically involves semester-long projects that focus on applying engineering principles to solve real-world problems or create innovative solutions. These projects are often interdisciplinary, requiring collaboration between students from different engineering disciplines, and they provide valuable hands-on experience in the engineering design process..

DT Projects

Design Thinking (DT) is a methodology used to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions by focusing on human-centered design, empathy, and iterative prototyping. In the context of academic programs, DT often involves students engaging in research, analysis, and solution development with a strong emphasis on creativity and user-centric design.

Course Projects

Course-relevant projects that aim to provide students with practical knowledge from their courses, the focus is typically on applying theoretical concepts learned in the classroom to real-world scenarios. These projects serve as opportunities for students to deepen their understanding, gain hands-on experience, and develop skills that are directly applicable to their field of study.
